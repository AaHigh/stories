Use the Source, Luke

A truth-seeking documentary treatment on the DMT experience, visible language & the glyph hypothesis

18 minute conceptual film • Single-file experience

The Core Claim

If the DMT phenomenon is objective rather than purely subjective hallucination, then the intricate glyphs and visible language structures repeatedly reported could, in principle, be recorded and decoded.

Testable hypothesis: Recurring glyph patterns should appear across independent experiencers above chance. A visible syntax or grammar should be detectable. Information should be extractable.

Why This Matters

Most psychedelic research stops at phenomenology or therapeutic outcomes. This hypothesis asks for something harder: operational decoding of the reported visible language.
