Sinners on Operation No Fly Zone

Local law enforcement success, state-level scrutiny, and federal enforcement in California's Central Valley.

Narrative non-fiction • July 2026 • Compiled from public records

In the spring of 2022, Fresno saw one of the largest coordinated law enforcement operations in its recent history. Called Operation No Fly Zone, it targeted violent street gangs — particularly the Flyboys and Hoover 107 — linked to multiple homicides, drive-by shootings, and human trafficking.

The months-long investigation involved the Fresno Police Department, Fresno County Sheriff’s Office, Fresno County District Attorney’s Office, California Department of Justice, FBI, Homeland Security Investigations, ATF, and other agencies.

By 2025–2026, a different set of stories had moved into public view involving California’s Attorney General Rob Bonta and related federal investigations into alleged bribery and corruption in the East Bay.

At the same time, federal immigration enforcement activity increased significantly in California’s Central Valley.
